Developers can voluntarily opt-in to this ecosystem by adding support to their App Store applications. This architectural shift is heavily motivated by Apple's recent 250 million USD class-action settlement over unfulfilled AI promises made during WWDC 2024. By acting as an orchestration layer, Apple controls the distribution and interface for AI services while offloading the astronomical capital expenditures and hallucination risks associated with massive frontier models onto its partners.
