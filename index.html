🔭 Overview

The U.S. government is moving to impose formal risk management standards on the use of AI in critical infrastructure, signaling a major shift from abstract principles to concrete, enforceable requirements.

The new profile will provide specific guidance for operators on managing AI risks, emphasizing deterministic behavior, graceful degradation, and robustness against adversarial attacks.
