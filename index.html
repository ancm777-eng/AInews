TITO bypasses this "molecular movie" bottleneck by learning underlying dynamics from short simulation sequences and then predicting molecular behavior over timescales a thousand times longer, even for processes it has never seen during training. The model was trained and validated against more than 12,500 organic molecules and 1,000 short peptides, with results perfectly consistent with known physics.
