The release has been hailed as a generational leap, enabling developers to generate infinite, physics-grounded synthetic video data to train physical AI policy models in days rather than months.

Intel and SambaNova serve as key infrastructure partners, providing the underlying hardware stack. The system runs on Intel Xeon 6 processors, SambaNova SN-40/SN-50 Reconfigurable Dataflow Units (RDUs), and NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs, with Together.ai signing on as the first commercial customer to deploy Intel's rack-scale reference design. The disaggregated inference blueprint optimizes the economics of large-scale agentic workloads by offloading the compute-heavy prefill phase to NVIDIA GPUs while utilizing SambaNova's dataflow architecture for the bandwidth-intensive decode phase.
