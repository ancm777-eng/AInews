While the Pax Silica Fund and the American AI Exports Program are administratively distinct — managed by different cabinet departments with different mandates — they are strategically complementary: the Commerce program creates the export channel, while the State Department fund finances the receiving infrastructure in partner countries. Together, they signal that AI infrastructure has moved beyond a commercial product to become a primary instrument of U.S. foreign policy.
