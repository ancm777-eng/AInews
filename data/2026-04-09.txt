## ✅ Recommended Approach

### Principles for Handling Date Uncertainty

> **The inability to confirm a precise date does not**
> **constitute sufficient grounds to abort a briefing.**

**Recommended Protocol:**
- **Explicitly disclose** the training data reference point
- **Proceed with the briefing** within the scope of available information
- **Transparently communicate** the potential absence of real-time data
